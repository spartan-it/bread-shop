— Our Story —

Started with one recipe.
Perfected over years.

The Daily Crust was born out of a simple frustration: why can't you get truly good bread at work? Not supermarket bread, not reheated bread — real bread, made with care, the same morning you eat it.

We mix and shape every loaf by hand. Our sourdough starter has been alive for three years. We use stoneground flour milled in small batches, and we never add anything you can't pronounce.

01
Mix & Ferment

Dough mixes at 9pm. Cold ferment overnight for deep flavour.

02
Shape by Hand

Each loaf shaped at 4am by our bakers. No machines, no shortcuts.

03
Stone Deck Bake

Into the oven at 5am. Out by 7am. At your desk by 8am.
