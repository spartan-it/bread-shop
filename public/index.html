— Our Story —

Born in a garage.
Perfected by obsession.

It started in 2019 with a backyard grill, a 15-pound brisket blend, and a stubbornness to never compromise on flavor. Today, Burgervault serves hundreds of burgers daily — but every single one is still made with the same obsessive care.

We source our wagyu from a single ranch in Texas. Our brioche buns are baked on-site every morning at 5 AM. Our sauces? Family secrets, legally protected.
